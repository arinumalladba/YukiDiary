Welcome!

This is a diary blog about a 1 year old Siamese cat named Yuki who currently resides in South London with his loving family of 7 human beings and one other cat called Luna. This page will give you all the updates about his adventures which includes harness training, playing fetch and of course having lots of naps. You can also see him in all the fun outfits his family loves to dress him in!

Featured Diary Entries

Yuki wearing a Khamis

This is in April 2024 after we returned from a trip to Saudi Arabia and we got him a traditional garment to wear. We bought him the smallest size and although they aren't supposed to be worn by cats, we thought he would look adorable in one. He was such a good sport when wearing it and actually got quite comfortable in it too! Doesn't he look so cute!!

Yuki coming home from the vet

This was in January 2024. Yuki had just come back from the vet after being neutered. Yuki doesn't do too well being outdoors but he does enjoy watching the outside world as long as he has one of humans in plain site. In this picture, he is wearing his "cone of shame" but it only benefitted him as it stopped him from messing around with the surgical site (barely!). As you can see he wasn't too pleased about wearing it. Poor Yuki!

Baby Yuki napping on top of his cat bed

This was in September 2023. We bought Yuki quite a few beds and a huge cat tree for him to rest in but he found this particular cat bed to be his favourite. This cat bed is supposed to be one of those that have the bed inside of the overall structure. However, Yuki preffered to sleep on top of the structure instead of inside of it. He was really comfy sleeping on top of it as you can see by his funny pose!
